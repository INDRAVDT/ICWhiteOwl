About Me

Indraraj Chatterjee is a results-driven banking executive with over 20 years of experience leading business execution, financial strategy, and enterprise transformation across global markets. He has successfully managed high-performing teams, multi-billion-dollar budgets, and complex operations in Wealth Management, Retail Banking, and Credit Cards. Indraraj is passionate about driving operational excellence through innovation, process optimization, and data-driven decision-making.

Currently serving as Director of Wealth Operations at Citi, he leads global operations execution, business analytics, and strategic initiatives across Wealth Management. He specializes in shaping executive operating cadences, streamlining client onboarding, and creating centralized business intelligence platforms that enhance real-time insights for business, operations, and regulatory reporting.

Prior to Citi, Indraraj held senior leadership roles across North America, Asia, and EMEA at Citi and JP Morgan, where he led cross-functional finance, analytics, and operations teams. His career highlights include delivering multi-million-dollar operational efficiencies, implementing enterprise KPI frameworks, and executing strategic initiatives that improved profitability, productivity, and customer outcomes.

With a global perspective and deep finance expertise, Indraraj combines analytical rigor with strategic storytelling to interpret markets, policy, and capital flows. IC White Owl is his platform to share insights on macroeconomics, market dynamics, and financial strategy for leaders and investors worldwide.
